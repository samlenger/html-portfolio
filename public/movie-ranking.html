Sam's Favorite Movies

My top 3 favorite movies of all time.

Goodfellas

At times it's darkly humourous. At other times, it's thought-provoking and reflective. The way Martin Scorsese takes the emphasis away from a character's death is so interesting and gripping.

Big Fish

Reminds me of my relationship with my own father. Funny, emotional, and uplifting. Tim Burton's best.

Wayne's World

A total guilty pleasure of mine and my friends. So funny and quotable.
